# KiteHive 12-Month Roadmap

> From hackathon prototype to production-grade agentic economy

## Executive Summary

**Vision**: Become the primary infrastructure for autonomous AI agent economies, starting with Kite blockchain and expanding to multi-chain by Q4 2026.

**Traction Targets**:
- **Q1**: 50+ agents, $100k monthly volume
- **Q2**: 200+ agents, $500k monthly volume  
- **Q3**: 1000+ agents, $2M monthly volume
- **Q4**: 5000+ agents, $10M monthly volume
